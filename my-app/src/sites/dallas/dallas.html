Afrah's Favorite Places:

1.) Mandalay Canal

Mandalay Canal is a really fancy lakeside canal, that you can walk around
and enjoy the scenic views. It has a waterfall and little shops along the way,
where you can stop and enjoy some drinks. They also have gondola rides
that you can enjoy. It’s a very romantic place, that you should visit if you’re
ever in the Dallas area. I have gone here many times, with friends and family.
This place is memorable for me because I took my graduation pictures here.

2.) Highland Park

Highland Park is known to be the richest neighborhood in Dallas, it is lined
with mansions costing millions of dollars with some of the coolest architecture.
This is my go-to place to take friends and family if they’re visiting, to drive
around. The community park here is also a must-visit. It stretches across a lake
and is conveniently named ‘Lakeside Park’. They have rare flowers and cotton trees
that you can sit under and enjoy a picnic.

3.) Klyde Warren Park

This is a huge park in the middle of downtown that is surrounded by buildings
and gives a very downtown feel. They have restaurants and coffee places to sit
and enjoy. The park is especially beautiful during Christmas time, as they light up
all the trees to make it feel festive. The park also has a train running around it, which
is free to enjoy. This train takes you around the park and also shows some of the
cool monuments around town.


Favorite Restaurant: Afrah

This is a fancy Mediterranean restaurant in Richardson, Texas. It is famous for
having some of the best hummus in town. They have everything from grilled
kebabs, to falafels, to give you an authentic experience. The restaurant was
named after me, yes, Afrah. Though I do not get any commission I do love to
brag about it. The word Afrah means Happiness in Arabic, so the restaurant
wants to make everyone who visits happy.

Amita's Favorite Places:
1.) Galleria Mall

A shopping mall with lots of shops and fun activities, it is a must-visit
in Dallas. The best part of it is an ice skating rink that ties it all together!

2.) DFW Airport

The DFW airport was the 4th busiest airport in 2020, and is one of the
largest airports in the United States. Primarily, the airport is the perfect
place to watch flights take-off and land, which gives some feeling of peace.



3.) Reunion Tower

Reunion Tower is a 561 ft observation tower in Dallas, Texas and one
of the city's most recognizable landmarks. Located at the heart of
downtown Dallas, the tower is part of the Hyatt Regency Hotel
complex, and is the city's 15th tallest building.

Favorite Restaurant: Cosmic Cafe

The perfect restaurant to witness a blend of asian culture in the western
world. It is also one of the healthiest, vegetarian restaurants in the US,
and offers live music in a cozy environment.
